Mohawk College ranked 2nd nationally, 1st provincially in applied research

Some good news for Mohawk College as provincial community colleges struggle with financial challenges brought about by cutbacks in foreign enrolment. Mohawk has been named as one of Canada’s top research colleges, ranking 2nd nationally in the 2025 Canada’s Top 50 Research Colleges list by Research Infosource.
