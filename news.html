Three colleges partner to offer a 4-year Business degree for tradespeople

First-of-its-kind online Bachelor of Business Administration (Trades Management) (Honours) offered by Algonquin, George Brown, Mohawk Algonquin College, George Brown College and Mohawk College have joined resources to launch a new four-year honours degree in Business Administration, focusing on people working in skilled trades occupations.
