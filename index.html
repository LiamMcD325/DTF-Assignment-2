Operated by the Mohawk College Radio Corporation and funded by Mohawk College, INDI 101.5 FM (CIOI) is Hamilton's Home for Alternative. The campus and community radio station features programming created by its Radio Broadcasting and Journalism students, along with a variety community-hosted specialty shows. There has been a student radio station in operation at Mohawk College for more than twenty-five years. At first the station was broadcast only to the student lounge adjacent to the studios.
