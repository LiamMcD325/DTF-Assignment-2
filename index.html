Operated by the Mohawk College Radio Corporation and funded by Mohawk College, INDI 101.5 FM (CIOI) is Hamilton's Home for Alternative. The campus and community radio station features programming created by its Radio Broadcasting and Journalism students, along with a variety community-hosted specialty shows.
